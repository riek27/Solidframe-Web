Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about construction website design and development

General Questions

Common questions about our process and services

How long does it take to build a construction website?

Our typical timeline is 4-6 weeks from project kickoff to launch. This includes design, development, content integration, and testing. Complex projects with additional features may take slightly longer. We provide a detailed timeline at the beginning of every project.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, we offer monthly maintenance plans starting at $99/month. These plans include security updates, regular backups, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical support. Many of our construction clients prefer this hands-off approach so they can focus on their business.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they look and function perfectly on smartphones and tablets. Given that over 60% of construction service searches happen on mobile, this is a top priority for us. We test on various devices to ensure optimal performance.

Can you help with photography for our projects?

While we don't provide photography services directly, we have partnerships with several professional photographers who specialize in construction and architectural photography. We can help coordinate this for you and provide guidance on what types of photos work best for construction websites.

How do you handle local SEO for construction companies?

We implement a comprehensive local SEO strategy that includes Google Business Profile optimization, local keyword targeting, citation building, location-specific content, and local backlink acquisition. This helps you appear in local searches when potential clients are looking for construction services in your area.

Technical Questions

Questions about the technical aspects of your website

What content management system (CMS) do you use?

We primarily use WordPress for construction websites because it's user-friendly, scalable, and has excellent SEO capabilities. However, we can also work with other platforms if you have specific requirements. We provide training on how to manage your content after launch.

Will I be able to update my website myself?

Yes, we build all our websites with easy-to-use content management systems that allow you to update text, images, and add new projects. We provide training and documentation to help you manage your site. For more complex updates, we offer maintenance plans or hourly support.

How do you ensure my website loads quickly?

We implement multiple optimization techniques including image compression, code minification, caching, and Content Delivery Network (CDN) integration. We aim for Google's recommended page load speed of under 3 seconds, which is crucial for both user experience and SEO.

Is website hosting included in your pricing?

Website hosting is not included in our design/development pricing, but we can help you set up reliable hosting. We recommend hosting providers that specialize in performance and security for business websites. Typical hosting costs range from $20-50/month depending on your needs.

Do you provide SSL certificates for security?

Yes, we include SSL certificate setup with every website we build. SSL encryption is essential for website security, protects user data, and is required by Google for good SEO rankings. Most hosting providers offer free SSL certificates through Let's Encrypt.

Pricing & Process Questions

Questions about costs, payments, and our workflow

What's included in your pricing packages?

Our packages include custom design, development, basic SEO setup, mobile optimization, content integration, training, and post-launch support (duration varies by package). Additional services like advanced SEO, content writing, and monthly maintenance are available as add-ons.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Typically, we require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion before launch. For larger projects, we can arrange a 3- or 4-payment plan. All payment options are discussed during the consultation phase.

What information do you need from me to get started?

We'll need information about your business, target audience, services, project examples, branding materials, and any specific features you want. We provide a detailed questionnaire and guide you through the content gathering process to make it as easy as possible.

How many revisions are included in the design phase?

We include 2-3 rounds of revisions in the design phase, depending on the package. This typically gives us enough flexibility to refine the design to your satisfaction while keeping the project on schedule. Additional revisions can be accommodated if needed.

What happens if I need changes after the website is launched?

All packages include a support period (30-180 days depending on the package) where we'll make minor adjustments at no additional cost. After the support period, you can make updates yourself, purchase individual update hours ($95/hour), or sign up for a monthly maintenance plan.
