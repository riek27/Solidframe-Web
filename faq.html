💬 General Questions

Common questions about our process and services

How long does it take to build a construction website?

+
Typically 2–4 weeks after homepage approval. The timeline depends on the complexity and number of pages. We provide a clear schedule during our discovery call.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

+
Yes! We offer monthly maintenance plans that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and content changes. It's the best way to keep your site secure and performing well.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+
Absolutely. Every site we build is fully responsive and optimized for smartphones and tablets. Over 60% of construction leads come from mobile devices, so we prioritize mobile performance.

Can you help provide high-quality images for our projects?

+
Yes, we can recommend professional photographers in Houston or guide you on capturing the best shots of your work. We also offer stock imagery optimization if needed.

How do you handle local SEO for construction companies?

+
We implement a comprehensive local SEO strategy: optimizing for Houston-specific keywords, setting up and enhancing your Google Business Profile, building local citations, and creating location pages for service areas.

⚙️ Technical Questions

Questions about the technical aspects of your website

What technologies do you use?

+
We build on modern, reliable platforms like WordPress (with custom themes) or static site generators for speed. All sites use HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript best practices.

Will you take care of my website after launch?

+
Yes, we offer post-launch support and ongoing maintenance plans. We're here to handle updates, security patches, and any issues that arise.

How do you ensure my website loads quickly?

+
We optimize images, leverage caching, minify code, and use fast hosting. Every site is tested with Google PageSpeed Insights to ensure sub‑2 second load times.

Is website hosting included in your pricing?

+
Hosting is offered as a separate monthly package (starting at $99/mo) which includes managed hosting, daily backups, and security monitoring. You're free to use your own hosting, but we recommend ours for seamless support.

Do you provide SSL certificates for security?

+
Yes, every website we launch includes a free SSL certificate to encrypt data and build trust with visitors (and Google).

💰 Pricing & Process Questions

Questions about costs, payments, and our workflow

What's included in your pricing packages?

+
Each package includes custom design, mobile optimization, SEO foundation, and the specified number of pages. See our pricing page for a detailed breakdown.

Do you offer payment plans?

+
Yes, we offer flexible options: typically 50% upfront / 50% on completion, or split into three monthly payments. Contact us to discuss what works best.

We'll need your logo (if you have one), high‑quality project photos, a list of services, and any existing branding guidelines. We guide you through the rest.
