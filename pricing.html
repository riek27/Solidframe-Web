FAQs About Pricing

Common questions about our construction website pricing

Why do construction websites cost more than generic websites?

Construction websites require industry-specific features like project galleries, service area maps, lead capture forms for estimates, and specialized SEO for local construction services. Our expertise in the construction industry ensures your website addresses the unique needs of your business and your clients.

Is there a monthly fee for the website?

Our pricing is primarily one-time for website design and development. The only ongoing costs would be for optional add-on services like monthly maintenance ($99+/month). Hosting is typically $99-$157/month depending on your needs, which we can help you set up.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most construction websites take 1-4 weeks from project kickoff to launch. The timeline depends on the package selected and how quickly we receive content (photos, project details, etc.) from you. We provide a detailed timeline at the start of every project.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Typically, we require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon completion before launch. Contact us to discuss payment options that work for your business.

What if I need changes after the website is launched?

All packages include a monthly plan where we'll make minor adjustments at no additional cost. sign up for a monthly maintenance plan.
