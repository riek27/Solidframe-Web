Answers to Common Questions

Why do construction websites cost more?

+
Construction and trade websites require industry-specific features like project galleries, lead capture forms, and local SEO targeting. We invest extra time to ensure your site generates actual jobs, not just looks pretty.

Is there a monthly fee?

+
Yes, each plan includes an optional hosting & maintenance package that covers secure hosting, daily backups, and ongoing updates. It's $99–$157/mo depending on the package.

How long does it take to build my site?

+
Typically 1–2 weeks after homepage approval. Premium projects may take 3–4 weeks due to additional custom features.

Do you offer payment plans?

+
Yes! We offer flexible payment options: 50% upfront / 50% on completion. Contact us for details.

Can I request changes after launch?

+
Absolutely. We’re here to support you even after your website goes live. You can request updates or changes at any time. These can be handled either through our standard hourly rate or covered under one of our ongoing maintenance plans. Our goal is to ensure your website continues to evolve with your business and consistently performs at a high level.
