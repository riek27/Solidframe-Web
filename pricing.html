Pricing FAQs

Common questions about our construction website pricing

What's the difference between the plans?

Starter is for small contractors needing basic online presence. Professional includes advanced features for established companies. Premium offers custom solutions for large construction firms with complex needs.

Are there any monthly fees?

No, our pricing is a one-time fee for design and development. Optional monthly services like SEO maintenance, hosting, and updates are available separately.

How long does it take to build my website?

Starter: 3-4 weeks, Professional: 4-6 weeks, Premium: 6-8 weeks. Timelines depend on content readiness and project complexity.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Typically 50% to start and 50% upon completion. We can discuss custom payment arrangements based on your needs.

What if I need changes after the website is complete?

We include support periods with each plan (30-180 days). After that, we offer maintenance plans or hourly rates for updates.

Do you provide hosting and domain?

We can help you set up hosting and domain registration (additional costs apply), or you can use your preferred provider. We provide recommendations.
