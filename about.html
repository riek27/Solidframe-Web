Our History

From a Houston Garage to Page One Rankings

Solidframe Web was born in 2017 when two Houston natives — a web developer and a digital marketer — saw a gap in the market. Local roofing companies, remodelers, and contractors were struggling with outdated, cookie‑cutter websites that failed to bring in leads. They set out to change that.

What started as a passion project helping a friend's roofing business quickly grew into Houston's go‑to web design agency for trade professionals. Today, we've delivered over 250 high‑performance websites, helping contractors across Harris County and beyond dominate search results and win more bids.
