About Solidframe Web

Specializing in premium website design for construction businesses across Texas & the USA

Building Digital Foundations for Construction Companies

Solidframe Web was born from a simple observation: most construction companies had outdated, ineffective websites that didn't reflect the quality of their work or help them win new business. We decided to change that by focusing exclusively on the construction industry.

Our team combines web development expertise with deep knowledge of construction marketing, enabling us to create websites that not only look impressive but also generate measurable results. We don't just build websites — we build digital assets that drive leads, showcase craftsmanship, and establish authority.

Serving construction businesses across the United States, we help contractors, builders, and remodelers establish a powerful online presence that drives growth, builds trust, and wins more projects in a competitive market.
