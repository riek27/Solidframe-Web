Refund Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with our services. Because we provide digital creative work, refunds are handled differently than physical products. This policy explains when refunds may be issued.

Deposits and Milestone Payments

Initial Deposit (50%): This deposit secures your spot in our schedule and covers initial strategy and design concepts. It is non‑refundable once work has begun (i.e., after we have started design mockups or development). If you cancel before any creative work has started, you may receive a full refund of the deposit, minus any administrative fees.

Final Payment: The remaining 50% is due upon completion of the project (before final files are handed over or the site goes live). This payment is also non‑refundable because it represents the finished work delivered to you.

Project Cancellation

If you decide to cancel a project after work has begun, you will be billed for the time and effort already invested (calculated at our standard hourly rate of $150/hour). Any remaining deposit balance will be refunded if it exceeds the value of work completed. No refunds will be given once the website has been delivered (even if not yet launched).

Dissatisfaction with Design Concepts

We offer two rounds of revisions on initial design concepts. If after those revisions you are still not satisfied, we may agree to part ways. In that case, you will own the concepts created up to that point, and we will refund any amount paid beyond the deposit, minus the value of work completed (at hourly rate).

SEO & Ongoing Services

SEO and monthly maintenance packages are billed monthly and are non‑refundable for any month in which services have been performed. You may cancel monthly services at any time with 30 days' written notice; no further charges will be made after cancellation.

Chargebacks

If you dispute a charge with your bank (chargeback) without first attempting to resolve the issue with us, we reserve the right to suspend all services and pursue collection of the disputed amount, plus any fees incurred.

How to Request a Refund

All refund requests must be sent in writing to billing@crestwebstudio.com. We will review your request and respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds will be processed via the original payment method and may take 5‑10 business days to appear.

Contact

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at info@crestwebstudio.com or call (713) 555-1234.
