Refund Policy

Simple & fair — last updated March 5, 2026

We want you to be happy with your website. Here’s how refunds work — it’s straightforward.

Deposit (50%)

When you start a project with us, we ask for a 50% deposit. This covers our time to plan, design, and build your custom homepage concept. If we haven’t started any design work yet, you can cancel and get a full refund. Once we’ve shown you a design concept, the deposit is non-refundable — because we’ve already put in the work.

Final Payment (50%)

The remaining 50% is due when your website is ready to launch. After you’ve seen the finished site and approved it, this payment is non-refundable. You’re getting a complete, working website that’s yours to keep.

What if you change your mind?

If you decide to cancel after work has begun, just let us know. We’ll total up the hours we’ve already spent (at a simple rate of $100/hour) and refund any leftover deposit amount. No hard feelings.

Not happy with the design?

We offer free revisions until you’re satisfied. If for some reason we can’t agree on a direction, we’ll refund any amount beyond the deposit, minus the time we’ve already invested. You’ll also own any designs we created up to that point.

Monthly SEO or maintenance plans

These are billed month-to-month. If you cancel, we won’t charge you for future months. But payments for months where we already did the work are non-refundable — because we showed up and did the job.

How to ask for a refund

Just email us at info@solidframeweb.com. Tell us why, and we’ll get back to you within 3 business days. If a refund is approved, we’ll send it back the same way you paid (usually within a week).

Questions?

We’re real people — reach out anytime. We’ll explain everything and make sure you feel comfortable before we start any project.

— The Solidframe Web team
