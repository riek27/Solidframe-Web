Privacy Policy

Simple & transparent — last updated March 5, 2026

At Crest Web Studio, we keep things simple. We respect your privacy and only collect what we need to help you with your website.

What we collect

When you reach out to us — whether by contact form, email, or phone — we may ask for your name, email address, and phone number. That’s it. We don’t ask for anything fancy.

Why we collect it

We use your info to get back to you, discuss your website project, and share how we can help your construction business grow online. We’ll never spam you or sell your info to anyone.

Cookies? Just the basics

Our website uses simple cookies to make things run smoothly (like remembering your preferences). You can turn them off in your browser if you like — no hard feelings.

Do we share your data?

Nope. We don’t sell, rent, or trade your personal information. The only time we might share it is if we’re required by law (but we’ll let you know first).

Your control

If you ever want us to delete your info or stop contacting you, just email us at info@crestwebstudio.com. We’ll handle it right away.

Questions?

We’re real people — just reach out. Call, email, or use our contact form. We’ll be happy to explain anything.

— The Crest Web Studio team
