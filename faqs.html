General Questions

What makes Crestline Web Studio different from other web agencies?

We specialize exclusively in the construction industry. Unlike general web agencies, we understand construction workflows, client concerns, and industry-specific marketing needs. Our team has worked with over 150 construction businesses, giving us deep insights into what works best for builders, contractors, and remodelers.

Do you only work with construction companies in Texas?

While we're based in Houston and love working with Texas construction businesses, we serve construction companies across the entire United States. Our expertise translates to any location, though we have particular insight into Texas markets and regulations.

How do you ensure my website will appeal to construction clients?

We use industry-specific research and our experience with construction clients to design websites that address their specific concerns. This includes clear project showcases, service explanations, credential displays, and trust-building elements that construction clients look for when hiring.

What if I need help after my website launches?

All our packages include a support period (30-180 days depending on package). After that, we offer maintenance plans starting at $99/month that include updates, security monitoring, backups, and technical support. We never leave you stranded after launch.

Services Questions

What exactly is included in "website design"?

Our website design service includes: custom layout creation, color scheme development, typography selection, construction-specific imagery selection, mobile-responsive design, user experience optimization, and industry-appropriate design elements that appeal to construction clients.

Do you handle website hosting and domain registration?

We can handle both hosting and domain registration for you (additional costs apply), or we can help you set these up with your preferred provider. We recommend reliable hosting services optimized for construction websites and can provide guidance on domain selection.

What is "local SEO" and why is it important for construction companies?

Local SEO optimizes your website to appear in location-based searches like "construction companies near me" or "roofing contractors in [your city]." Since most construction work is local, this is crucial for attracting clients in your specific service areas. We optimize your Google Business Profile, build local citations, and implement location-specific keywords.

Can you redesign my existing construction website?

Yes, website redesign is one of our specialty services. We'll audit your current site, identify areas for improvement, and create a modern, high-performing website while preserving your existing content and SEO value. Many construction businesses see significant improvements after a redesign with us.

Pricing & Payments

Why is construction website design more expensive than generic templates?

Custom construction websites require specialized knowledge of the industry, custom functionality for project showcases, specific lead capture forms for construction services, and optimization for local search in competitive markets. Our expertise ensures your website generates real business results, not just looks pretty.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. Typically we require 50% to start the project and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. We understand cash flow is important for construction businesses and work to create payment terms that work for you.

Are there any hidden fees?

No, our pricing is transparent. The quoted price includes design, development, basic SEO setup, and the support period. Additional costs would only be for optional add-ons like premium stock photography, custom illustrations, extended SEO services, or ongoing maintenance plans - all discussed and approved upfront.

What if I want to add features later?

We can always add features later. During our support period, minor additions are often included. After that, we offer hourly rates or project quotes for new features. We recommend planning your essential features upfront but understand that business needs evolve.

Process & Timeline

What is your typical website development process?

Our process: 1) Discovery & strategy session, 2) Design mockups and approval, 3) Development and content integration, 4) Testing and revisions, 5) Launch and SEO setup, 6) Training and handover. We maintain clear communication throughout and provide regular updates.

How long does it take to build a construction website?

Timelines vary by package: Starter (3-4 weeks), Professional (4-6 weeks), Premium (6-8 weeks). The biggest factor in timeline is how quickly you can provide content (text, photos, project details). We'll create a detailed timeline during our initial planning.

What do you need from me to get started?

We need: your business information, logo and branding materials, project photos, service descriptions, company bio, and any existing content you want to keep. We provide a detailed checklist and can help you gather what you need.

How many revisions do I get?

We include 2 rounds of revisions for design and 2 rounds for development. This covers most needs while keeping the project on schedule. Additional revisions are available if needed. Our goal is to get it right within the included revisions.

Technical Questions

Will my website work on mobile devices?

Absolutely. All our websites are built "mobile-first," meaning they're designed for mobile devices first then enhanced for desktop. Given that over 60% of construction service searches happen on mobile, this is a critical feature we prioritize for every site.

What platforms/technologies do you use?

We use modern, standards-based technologies: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and optionally WordPress for content management. We avoid bloated frameworks that slow down sites. Our focus is on fast, secure, and maintainable code that delivers excellent performance.

How do you ensure website security?

We implement SSL certificates, follow security best practices in coding, use secure hosting environments, and provide regular security updates as part of our maintenance plans. For WordPress sites, we implement additional security measures and monitoring.

Can I update the website content myself?

Yes! We build websites with easy-to-use content management systems (like WordPress) so you can update text, add project photos, and manage your content without technical knowledge. We provide training and documentation to ensure you're comfortable making updates.

Industry Specific

How do you showcase construction projects effectively?

We create dedicated project galleries with filtering options (by type, location, year), before/after comparisons, detailed project descriptions, and high-quality photography layouts. We understand that your projects are your best marketing tool and design accordingly.

What construction-specific features do you include?

Service area maps, project timeline displays, certification/credential showcases, team member profiles with specialties, client testimonial sections, service request forms tailored to construction needs, and integration with construction-specific software when needed.

How do you handle service area targeting for construction companies?

We create service area pages, optimize for local keywords, set up location-specific content, implement schema markup for service areas, and ensure your Google Business Profile is properly configured. This helps you attract clients in your specific geographic markets.

Do you understand construction industry regulations and certifications?

Yes, we're familiar with common construction certifications (LEED, OSHA, state licenses), insurance requirements, and industry regulations. We know how to prominently display these trust signals on your website to build credibility with potential clients.
