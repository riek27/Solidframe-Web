Our Collaborative Process

From our first video call to your final approval — we build with transparency and craftsmanship

How We Build Your Construction Website

We involve you at every stage. After each milestone, we share the work via video call or a private link — and we don't move forward until you're 100% happy.

01

Discovery & Strategy

We hop on a video call to understand your business, audience, and goals. We’ll review your competitors and map out the site structure together.

02

Design Concept + Homepage

We design a custom homepage concept based on our strategy. We share it with you (via video or link) for feedback. Once approved, we proceed.

You approve before we build further
03

Development & Inner Pages

After homepage sign‑off, we develop the full site (About, Services, Portfolio, etc.). We send you a staging link so you can see it come to life.

04

Review, Revise & Launch

You review the entire site, request any changes, and give final approval. We handle the launch and make sure everything runs smoothly.

Every step includes a video call or a shareable preview. You're never left guessing — we move forward only when you say “go”.
