What Happens at Each Stage

More Than Just a Timeline — It's a Partnership

1

Discovery Call

We schedule a Zoom meeting to learn about your business, competitors, and goals. This is where we identify the keywords and features that will drive leads for your specific trade.

2

Homepage Design & Approval

We design a custom homepage that reflects your brand and is built to convert. You'll receive a live preview link to review and request changes. We iterate until you're 100% satisfied.

3

Full Development

With the homepage locked in, we build the rest of your site — interior pages, contact forms, galleries, and any custom functionality. We keep you updated every step of the way.

4

Launch & Optimization

We push your site live, then immediately optimize: speed tests, mobile checks, and on‑page SEO (titles, meta descriptions, schema). We ensure Google can find and rank your site for Houston searches.

5

Ongoing Support & Growth

After launch, we remain your digital partner. We offer website updates, SEO improvements, performance tracking, and strategy calls to help you get more leads over time. Many clients stay with us on affordable monthly plans for continuous growth.
